Advancing Knowledge

Where academia meets real-world application

Whitefield Research and Innovation is a forward-thinking academic organisation based in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, dedicated to providing high-quality, expert-led training programmes that bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical industry skills.

Recognised by the Ministry of MSME, Government of India, and a proud member of the Microsoft for Startups Founders Hub and the Google Developer Program, WRI is committed to building the next generation of technology professionals.
